"The Green Brew Bros Story"

From a small home brewery to the largest private brewery in Germany

We are Green Brew Bros. We are a small microbrewery established in 2018 in the great province of Nova Scotia. Two brothers, Simon and Samuel, and a love of ales is how our story begins. Our beers are all organic and have a very earthy feel. Our bottles are all eco-friendly, reusable and biodegradable. The most important thing for us is to make beer while keeping mother earth happy.
